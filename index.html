DIVEST THE DIRT

Når du er ute i naturen, på vei ned en slalombakke eller joggende i marka, tenker du på hva din bank gjør med pengene dine?

Altfor mange banker fortsetter å investere penger i aktiviteter knyttet til fossilt brensel som setter vår planet og vår fremtid på spill. Og det er godt mulig at banken din er en av de. Dette må ta slutt.

WILL YOU JOIN US?

Vi, som en del av den globale POW Divest the Dirt kampanjen, ønsker å bidra til at stemmene til det norske friluftssamfunnet blir hørt angående investeringer knyttet til utvinning av fossilt brensel i Arktis. Flere norske banker fortsetter å investere våre penger i selskaper som driver med utvinning i Arktis.

En studie gjort av norske EtiskBankGuide viser at kun to norske banker har fullstendig solgt seg ut av denne typer aktiviteter. Globalt har kommersielle banker bidratt med 314 milliarder dollar til å utvide utvinning av fossilt brensel i Arktis. Det er avgjørende at vi øker presset på bankene våre og ber de lede an veien mot en lavkarbonfremtid. Vi mener at:
  • Våre penger ikke skal finansiere prekære aktiviteter for fossilt brensel i det skjøre arktiske økosystemet
  • Bankene bør være transparente på hvor stor andel av deres investeringer som er knyttet til miljø- og sosialt skadelige aktiviteter.

Med COP26 rett rundt hjørnet må vi kreve at bankenes investeringer samsvarer med deres løfter i henhold til Parisavtalen. De må ha en plan for utfasing av investeringer i fossilt brensel hvis vi skal ha håp om å nå 1,5 graders målet.

Vi ber deg ta kontakt med banken din og spørre om de investerer i arktisk fossilt brensel. Din stemme og dine penger har makt. Sammen kan vi beskytte naturen vår og bygge en bærekraftig fremtid som vi ønsker å leve i.

Ta kontakt med din bank i dag og finn ut hva de gjør med dine penger. Har de bestemt seg for å avhende sine porteføljer fra arktisk fossilt brensel? Hvis ikke, hvorfor har de ikke gjort det enda?

COUNT ME IN! SO WHAT DO WE DO??

Despite pressure to shift investments to sustainable options, the world’s biggest banks continue to increase their financing into fossil fuel activities. In 2020 alone, 17 European banks put nearly €162 billion EUR into fossil fuel companies. Banks need to change course immediately. The future of the outdoors depends on it.

With COP26 around the corner, we must demand that banks’ investments match their Paris Agreement promises. They need to have a fossil fuel phase-out plan if there’s any hope of meeting the 1.5°C target. We need you to ask your bank if they are investing in fossil fuels. Your voice matters and your money matters. Together we can protect the great outdoors and build a sustainable future we want to live in.

Write to your bank today to find out what they do with your money and if they have a fossil fuel phase-out plan.

Why Climate Finance? Why Now?

The latest and most stark warning of our climate breakdown from the 2021 IPCC Report makes it clear that immediate action needs to be taken. In order to meet the 1.5°C target in the Paris Agreement, there should be no more development of new oil, gas, or coal beyond 2021 (International Energy Agency). The time is up for allowing our banks to fund fossil fuel projects without our consent.

With COP26 on the horizon, all eyes will be on world leaders to make bold climate decisions. One of the big agenda items for COP is climate finance, so we’re seizing this opportunity to put pressure on financial institutions. The decisions made at COP26 will affect the future of all our lives and the outdoor places we love.

The EU Commission adopted an Action Plan for Financing Sustainable Growth. As part of this plan, the Sustainable Finance Disclosure Regulation (SFDR), the Low Carbon Benchmarks Regulation and the new EU Taxonomy impose mandatory ESG (Environmental, Social, Governance) disclosure obligations for financial institutions. This requires all financial institutions to disclose to what extent they are associated with sustainable economic activity.

This is happening soon, giving us a small window of opportunity to show banks that we’re paying attention. Their disclosures will not go unnoticed and we have high expectations. You have the power to influence your bank and let them know this matters to you and that you will give your money only to the greenest bank. Find out what your bank is doing with your money.

A GLOBAL POW MOVEMENT

The Protect Our Winters network is leveraging its global outdoor community  and campaigning together for climate finance change. Giving those who ski, hike, climb, or ride bikes the tools to take action and empower them to use their money to help finance a greener world.

POW US is focusing on the financial portfolios of outdoor brands, because greening their financial supply chain is actually one of the most powerful things a company can do for the planet.

POW Canada is targeting Canada’s five largest banks through their #BankingOnNature campaign.

POW UK is demanding local councils divest their pensions, because 75% of local UK councils declared a climate crisis, they collectively hold investments of nearly £10 billion in sketchy fossil fuel funds.

POW Norway is focusing on retail banks’ financing of fossil fuel exploration in the Arctic Circle.

POW Europe (along with 7 continental POW chapters) is targetting retail banks.

Source: Banking on Climate Chaos (RAN 2021)

WHAT ELSE CAN I DO?

Have you sent the letter to your bank, shared the message and looked into changing to a greener bank and now wonder what more you can do? If you want to have an even bigger impact than sending a mail, consider calling your bank or go to your bank and ask to speak to a manager and express your concerns.

We’d like to hear what your bank has to say about your concerns and questions. Send us the responses you receive to campaigns@protectourwinters.eu.

We have plenty of other ideas what you can do to make your money greener. If you opted in to our newsletter, we will be back in touch a few weeks after this first phase of the campaign to talk about what steps you can take next.
